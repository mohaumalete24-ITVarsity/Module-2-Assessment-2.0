SPUR

Spur Corporation (doing business as Spur Steak Ranches) (JSE:SUR) is a steakhouse franchise restaurant chain originating from South Africa with a focus on family dining. The head office of Spur Corporation is situated in Centurity City, Cape Town. Although the founding and flagship brand is the Spur steakhouse restarants,the company owns a number of other, predominantly South Africa, restaurant brands. Spur Steak Ranches is a themed South African family favourite and is fondly known as "the official restaurant of the South African family".
